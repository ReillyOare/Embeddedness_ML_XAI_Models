These datasets were collected by inputting point shapefiles of each VA DEQ embeddedness site into the USGS StreamStats Batch Processor (https://www.usgs.gov/tools/streamstats-batch-processing-tool).

Metadata can be found at: https://streamstats.usgs.gov/information-portal/statistics.html

The '_TRIMMED1' datasets were manually trimmed in Excel due to excess of information. Trimming was conducted based on professional opinion.
